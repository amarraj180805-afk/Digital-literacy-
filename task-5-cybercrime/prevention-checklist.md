#  **Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (India)**

## **General Safety Tips**
- **Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts**  
- **Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible**  
- **Always verify website URLs before entering sensitive information**  
- **Avoid clicking on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or social media**  
- **Download apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or App Store**  
- **Keep your device, apps, and antivirus software updated regularly**  

---

##  **Financial / UPI Safety Tips**
- **Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone**  
- **Do not approve unknown payment requests (Collect requests) on UPI apps**  
- **Always double-check receiver details before sending money**  
- **Avoid scanning random QR codes from unknown sources**  

---

##  **Reporting Cybercrime in India**
- **Report incidents on the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in**  
- **Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (for immediate financial fraud reporting)**  

---

## **Summary**
**By following these simple yet effective precautions, college students can protect themselves from cyber threats such as phishing, fraud, and identity theft. Awareness and quick action are key to staying safe online.**
