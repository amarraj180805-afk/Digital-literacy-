#  Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

## Do’s
- **Think before posting anything online to avoid misunderstandings**
- **Maintain respectful and polite communication with others**
- **Use privacy settings to protect your personal information**
- **Share accurate, useful, and verified information**
- **Build a positive and professional online presence**

##  Don’ts
- **Do not share fake news or unverified information**
- **Avoid posting offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content**
- **Do not engage in cyberbullying or unnecessary online arguments**
- **Avoid sharing personal or sensitive information publicly**
- **Do not copy or misuse someone else’s content without permission**

##  Summary
Following proper social media etiquette helps maintain a positive digital identity, ensures safe communication, and prevents misunderstandings in online interactions.
